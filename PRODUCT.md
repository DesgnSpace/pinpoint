# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Designers and developers who need exact pixel coordinates from images — slicing mockups, writing CSS/image maps, QA layout testing. They're already using browser DevTools or pixel-ruler apps. Pinpoint replaces the multi-tool workflow with one focused drop-and-click.

## Product Purpose

A fast, precise tool to get pixel coordinates and position data from any image. Upload or paste an image, click anywhere to get X/Y positions in pixels and percentages, view image metadata (dimensions, aspect ratio, EXIF), and copy any value to clipboard. No accounts, no ads, no cruft.

## Brand Personality

Precise, minimal, sharp. Utility-first. A surgical instrument, not a Swiss Army knife.

## Anti-references

No over-designed tools. Avoid heavy dashboards with charts, gradients, glassmorphism, or visual decoration. Not a "creative suite" — a single-purpose tool that respects the user's attention.

## Design Principles

- **Precision before polish** — accuracy is the product. Every visual choice serves measurement clarity.
- **Zero friction** — upload, click, copy. No accounts, no onboarding, no settings.
- **Surgical clarity** — every element earns its place. If it doesn't serve the task, it doesn't exist.
- **Trust the expert** — designers and developers recognize craft in restraint. Sparse, direct, no hand-holding.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Sufficient contrast ratios, visible focus indicators, keyboard-navigable controls.
